Ankit Singh Patyal, known by his stage name Ikka, is an Indian rapper, lyricist, and composer. Born in New Delhi, India, Ikka began his musical journey in the early 2000s and rose to prominence with his unique blend of Hindi and Punjabi lyrics. He was a member of the hip-hop collective Mafia Mundeer, alongside artists like Raftaar, Badshah, and Yo Yo Honey Singh. Over the years, Ikka has made significant contributions to Bollywood music and has released several solo tracks and albums.
