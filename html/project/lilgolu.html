Lil Golu is an Indian rapper, lyricist, and composer known for his contributions to the Hindi and Punjabi music scenes. He has collaborated with prominent artists like Yo Yo Honey Singh, IKKA, and Jazzy B. Some of his notable tracks include "Issey Kehte Hain Hip Hop," "Stardom," "Gaanewale Rapper," and "13 14." Lil Golu's music blends traditional Indian sounds with contemporary hip-hop elements, creating a unique and energetic style.
