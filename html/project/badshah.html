Aditya Prateek Singh Sisodia, better known by his stage name Badshah, is an Indian rapper, singer, songwriter, and music producer. Born on November 19, 1985, in New Delhi, India, he has made significant contributions to the Indian music industry. Badshah has recorded his songs in Hindi, Punjabi, English, and Haryanvi languages. He is known for his chart-topping hits and has been a prominent figure in the Indian music scene.
